# 1-on-1 Mentorship & Check-in Agenda

## Engineer: [Name]
## Manager: Joshua Olatunji
## Frequency: [Weekly / Bi-Weekly]

---

### Philosophy
This meeting belongs to the Engineer, not the Manager. It is not a status update for Jira tickets. It is dedicated time to discuss career growth, technical blockers, team dynamics, and mentorship.

---

## 1. Pulse Check (5 mins)
- How are you feeling this week on a scale of 1-5? (1 = Burned out, 5 = Fantastic)
- What is currently taking up most of your mental energy?

## 2. Blockers & Frustrations (10 mins)
- Is there any process, tooling, or cross-team dependency that is slowing you down?
- Is there a technical concept or architectural decision you are struggling with? Let's whiteboard it.

## 3. Career Growth & Mentorship (10 mins)
- What is one technical skill or leadership skill you want to improve this month?
- Are you getting enough challenging work? Or too much?
- *Manager Feedback:* Highlight one thing the engineer did exceptionally well this week, and one area for constructive improvement.

## 4. Action Items (5 mins)
- **Manager to-do:** [e.g., Unblock the AWS permissions issue]
- **Engineer to-do:** [e.g., Draft the RFC for the cache migration]
