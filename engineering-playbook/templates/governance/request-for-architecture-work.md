# Request for Architecture Work (RAW)

*This template is used by Product Managers or Business Stakeholders to formally request technical architecture design for a new initiative.*

*   **Project Title:** [e.g., Dependly Fraud Detection Engine]

## 2. Business Problem & Objectives
*Do not prescribe a technical solution here. What is the business pain point?*
*   **Problem:** [e.g., We are losing $10k/month to chargebacks because we only review transactions manually.]
*   **Objective:** [e.g., Automatically flag and block high-risk transactions before authorization.]

## 3. High-Level Requirements (Functional)

1. [e.g., Must provide an internal dashboard for the Risk team to review flagged transactions.]

## 4. Key Constraints & Budget
